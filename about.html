Our story

One lawyer. One breaking point. One firm.

Adrian Meridian spent years as one of the country's most gifted defense attorneys — inside a justice system where 99% of criminal trials end in conviction. He watched innocent people plead guilty because fighting felt impossible.

In 2018 he walked out of a courtroom, and into his own practice — built around a single promise: every client gets a real trial, not a formality. Today, Meridian Law & Associates is where the counted-out come to be heard.
